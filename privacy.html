Privacy Policy

scope.inc · Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. Overview

scope.inc ("we", "us", or "our") operates Agent Scope, an internal content management tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information required to operate the Service:

  • TikTok account identifiers and OAuth access tokens for authorized accounts
  • YouTube channel data and video metadata for authorized accounts
  • Video files and associated metadata uploaded through the Service

3. How We Use Information

Information is used exclusively to:

  • Publish video content to authorized TikTok and YouTube accounts
  • Retrieve channel analytics and comment data for internal reporting
  • Operate and improve the Service for internal use

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties. Data is shared with TikTok and YouTube only as necessary to deliver the core functionality of the Service (video publishing, analytics retrieval).

5. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored locally on secured internal systems and are deleted upon revocation or when access is no longer required. Video metadata is retained for internal record-keeping.

6. Security

We implement reasonable technical measures to protect information processed by the Service. Access is restricted to authorized scope.inc personnel only.

7. Your Rights

Authorized users may request deletion of their data at any time by contacting us. TikTok users can revoke app access at any time through TikTok's settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
